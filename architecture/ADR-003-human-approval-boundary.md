# ADR-003: Human approval boundary

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

AMAS may recommend `ready_for_human_approval`, revisions, insufficient evidence, policy escalation, or a security block. It may not formally approve an assessment. Only an authorised human review action changes the final institutional state.

## Rationale

Assessment approval carries academic, legal, accessibility, and quality-assurance consequences. Model confidence and a second-model critic do not establish accountable authority.
