# Synthetic Accessible Assessment Design Standard

**Source ID:** ACCESS-003  
**Version:** 1.0-demo  
**Status:** Active (demonstration corpus only)  
**Authority rank:** 70

## 3. Clarity
Assessment instructions must use explicit verbs, identify required evidence, explain submission formats, and avoid contradictory requirements.

## 4. Technology access
An assessment must not assume access to paid software, high-performance devices, continuous connectivity, or a specific AI subscription unless the institution provides equitable access or an approved alternative.

## 5. Reasonable adjustments
Moderation must identify avoidable barriers but must not infer a student's disability or prescribe an individual adjustment. Such decisions remain with authorised staff under the institution's process.
